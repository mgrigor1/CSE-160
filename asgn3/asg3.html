Greetings welcome to the "Cow & Coins", please find all the coins, when done come back to the cow to brag!

Walk around using keys: W to move forward, S to move backwards, A to move left and D to move right. Use keys: Q to pan camera to the left and E to pan camera to the right (also pans on mouse down).

Use B to add a block and R to remove a block (get closer to the desired block!).

Have fun and look for the coins in the maze!!
